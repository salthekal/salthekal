### What I'm working on

I've been thinking a lot lately about what it means for machine learning to move from research notebooks into operational systems where it actually gets used. In my current role I get to see the uncooperative side of that problem — messy multi-platform data, regulatory constraints that change mid-quarter, and the fact that the best model is worthless if the people who need it can't trust it. On the research side, my LUXE work gave me a healthy respect for the kind of careful error propagation and uncertainty quantification that the operations world usually ignores. Bridging those two sensibilities is the thread running through most of what I build.
